FAQ

Straight answers.

How long does it take?
Starter sites go live within 7 days of you approving the mock-up. Pro sites within 14. The most common delay is waiting on photos — we'll help you sort those.
Do I need the monthly care plan?
No — it's optional. Without it you'll just need to arrange your own hosting (we'll set it up and hand everything over). Most clients take it so they never have to think about the website again.
I already have a website. Can you fix it instead?
Usually it's faster and cheaper to rebuild on our system than patch an old site — and the result is far better. Your domain, email and Google reviews all carry over.
Who writes the words and takes the photos?
We write all the copy from a short chat with you. Photos of your work from your phone are usually perfect — we'll tell you exactly what to snap.
Will I actually show up on Google?
Every site is built with local search in mind, and your Google Business profile is set up properly — that alone puts you ahead of most local competitors. Rankings build over weeks, not overnight, and we report progress monthly on the care plan.
What if I want changes later?
Small changes are included free for the first month, and unlimited on the care plan. Bigger additions are quoted as a fixed price before any work starts.
